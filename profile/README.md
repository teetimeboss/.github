# Tee Time BOSS ⛳️

**Your tee times, handled.**

Tee Time BOSS helps golfers spend less time refreshing booking pages and more time playing golf.

Tell us where you play, when you like to tee off, how many players are in your group, and we’ll help automate the rest.

## What we do

Golfers love playing golf.

They do **not** love waking up early, refreshing club booking systems, racing other members for tee times, or missing out on their preferred slots.

That’s where Tee Time BOSS comes in.

We help golfers:

- Find supported golf clubs
- Set preferred playing days and time windows
- Configure tee time booking preferences
- Automate booking requests
- Track booking activity
- Get updates when action is needed

Simple, useful, and built for golfers.

## Why Tee Time BOSS?

Because getting a tee time should not feel like fighting for concert tickets.

Tee Time BOSS is designed for golfers who want:

- ⏰ Less time refreshing tee sheets
- 🏌️ More time on the course
- 📅 Better control over preferred playing times
- 🔔 Clear updates when something happens
- 🧘 A simpler way to manage club bookings

## Built around golfers

Tee Time BOSS is made for real golfers, real clubs, and real booking headaches.

Whether you play every weekend, chase early morning slots, organise regular groups, or just want a better chance of getting your preferred tee time, our goal is to make the process easier.

## Clubs and availability

We support a growing list of golf clubs and booking systems.

Can’t find your club?

No problem — you can request it and we’ll review whether Tee Time BOSS can support the club’s tee sheet.

## Our mission

To make tee time booking easier, faster, and less frustrating for golfers everywhere.

Golf should start on the first tee, not with repeatedly refreshing a booking screen.
